About me

My name is Xingche Guo (郭星澈). I'm a 4th year Ph.D student in Department of Statistics at Iowa State University advised by Dr. Dan Nettleton, Dr. Somak Dutta, and Dr. Yehua Li. My current research interests are high-dimensional functional data analysis, Bayesian statistics, spatial statistics, and image analysis using machine learning and deep learning algorithms.
